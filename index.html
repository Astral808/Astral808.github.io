About Me

Hello everyone! This is my first website, and I have absolutely no idea what I'm doing, but somehow its working! To start it off, I am James Sarol, and I am a weather forecaster for the United States Air Force. I have been in the Air Force a couple years, and I am nearing the end of my enlistment. I'm still not sure what I want to do, but I have been leaning towards re-enlisting.
